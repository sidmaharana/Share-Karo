Need Help?

Welcome to the ShareKaro Help Center. If you're experiencing any issues or have questions, you're in the right place.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I upload a file? - Click "SELECT FILES" button, choose your files, and wait for upload to complete.
  • What types of files are supported? - All file types are supported with no restrictions.
  • How long are files stored? - Files are automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • Is my data safe? - Yes, all files are encrypted end-to-end with AES-256 encryption.
  • What's the maximum file size? - Currently 2GB per file.
