Shipping & Delivery

ShareKaro is a digital file and text sharing platform. As such, there are no physical products to ship or deliver. All our services are provided digitally and are accessible instantly upon use.

Digital Delivery

When you upload a file or share text, it is made available digitally through a unique access code. The recipient can then download the file or retrieve the text directly from our platform using this code.

Access & Availability

Our services are available globally, 24/7, subject to internet connectivity and server availability. There are no geographical restrictions on accessing or using ShareKaro.

Data Expiration

Please note that all shared files and text have an automatic expiration time (3 minutes for files, 10 minutes for text). This is a core feature designed for privacy and efficient resource management, not a delivery constraint.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding the digital delivery of our services or any other inquiries, please contact us at support@sharekaro.com.
