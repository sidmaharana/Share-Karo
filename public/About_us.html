About Share Karo

ShareKaro is more than just a file-sharing platform; it's a commitment to secure, intuitive, and privacy-first digital interactions. Born from a passion for seamless data exchange, our platform is meticulously crafted by a team of dedicated developers who believe that sharing information should be simple, fast, and unequivocally secure.

Our core mission is to provide lightning-fast file and text transfers without ever compromising on security. We achieve this through the rigorous implementation of end-to-end encryption and a robust zero-knowledge architecture. This means your data is encrypted on your device before it even leaves your control, and only the intended recipient can decrypt it. We never store your unencrypted data, ensuring your privacy remains paramount.

With its distinctive matrix-themed user interface, minimalistic design philosophy, and a powerful, resilient backend infrastructure, ShareKaro offers a truly unique and efficient experience. Whether you're a student collaborating on projects, a developer sharing code snippets, or a professional exchanging sensitive documents, ShareKaro is designed to meet your needs with unparalleled reliability.

Beyond just files, our platform also supports instant text sharing, allowing for quick and secure exchange of messages or notes. Both file and text shares come with automatic expiration timers (5 minutes for files, 10 minutes for text), ensuring that your data doesn't linger unnecessarily on our servers, further enhancing your privacy and data hygiene.

Technology Behind ShareKaro

ShareKaro is built on a robust and modern technology stack designed for performance, scalability, and security:

  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js for a fast, scalable, and efficient server-side application.
  • Database: MongoDB, leveraging GridFS for efficient storage and retrieval of large files, and standard collections for text data.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Deployed on a resilient cloud platform to ensure high availability and global accessibility.
  • Frontend: HTML5, Tailwind CSS for a responsive and modern design, and vanilla JavaScript for dynamic interactions.
  • Security: Implementation of AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit, coupled with secure coding practices.
