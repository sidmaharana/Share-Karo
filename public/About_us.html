About Share Karo

Share Karo is a secure, intuitive, and privacy-first file sharing platform built by passionate developers who believe in simple, secure file transfers.

Our mission is to provide lightning-fast file transfer without compromising security. We leverage end-to-end encryption and a zero-knowledge architecture to make sure your data remains private and in your control.

With our innovative matrix-themed UI, minimalistic approach, and robust backend architecture, Share Karo offers a unique experience for students, developers, and professionals alike.

The platform supports both file and text sharing with automatic expiration after 24 hours, ensuring your data doesn't linger unnecessarily on our servers.
