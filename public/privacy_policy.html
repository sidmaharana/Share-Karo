Privacy Policy

At ShareKaro, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services. This includes:

  • Files and Text: When you upload files or share text, this content is temporarily stored on our servers. We implement a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning your data is encrypted on your device before it reaches us, and we do not have the means to decrypt it.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable information about how you interact with our service, such as access times and features used, to improve our service.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the ShareKaro service:

  • To facilitate secure file and text transfers.
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and service functionality.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our service.

3. Data Retention

For your privacy and to manage storage efficiently, all uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after 3 minutes from the time of upload. Shared text snippets are stored for 10 minutes. We do not retain any user content beyond these specified durations.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-leading security measures, including AES-256 end-to-end encryption, to protect your data. Your files and text are encrypted on your device before transmission and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient. We do not have access to your unencrypted content.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may share non-identifiable, aggregated usage data with trusted third parties for analytics purposes, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at maharana0418@gmail.com.
