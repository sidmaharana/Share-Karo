Cancellation & Refund Policy

At ShareKaro, we primarily offer a free service for file and text sharing. As such, there are generally no direct monetary transactions that would require a traditional cancellation or refund policy for our core services.

However, if you have made a voluntary donation or purchased any premium features (if applicable in the future), this policy would apply.

Donations

Donations made to ShareKaro are considered voluntary contributions to support our service and are generally non-refundable. We appreciate your generosity in helping us maintain and improve ShareKaro.

Premium Features (Future)

Should ShareKaro introduce premium features or paid subscriptions in the future, the following general principles would apply:

  • Cancellation: You would typically be able to cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellations would usually take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  • Refunds: Refunds for premium features would be handled on a case-by-case basis, depending on the nature of the feature and the terms of service at that time. Generally, refunds would not be provided for partial usage periods.

Contact Us

If you believe you are eligible for a refund or have any questions regarding a payment, please contact us at billing@sharekaro.com. Please provide your transaction details and the reason for your inquiry.
